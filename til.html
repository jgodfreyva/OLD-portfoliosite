Today I Learned

Wed Jan 2, 2019

Today I dug into Loretta Lynn's tunes. What an incredible musician. Learned that she got married at 15 and had 4 kids by the time she was 20. She went on to become the most awarded female artist in country music but radio stations used to regularly ban her songs because they were too controversial. You're cool, Loretta.
